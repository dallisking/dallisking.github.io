Hello Harmony

Dallis King, Samantha Puder, Nichie Enriquez

November 2014

Hello Harmony is an application written in processing for an android device. Its purpose is to be used as a grounding technique for those who suffer anxiety attacks by giving them power to control the application. The application plays soothing music and produces constant, spherical shapes to provide a means of mindfulness meditation that a person could take with them anywhere they went.

The appilcation is control through swiping, tapping and tilting the device. Each allows the user to control the speed, size and colours that appear on the screen.
