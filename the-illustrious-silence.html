The Illustrious Silence

Dallis King

April 2014

Created in processing, The Illustrious Silence was built to be a visual representation of alphabetical data, taken from a series of my favourite quotes and books, in an attempt to visualize my feelings for these words. I built algorithms to draw lines and circles based on the order of letters, and presented it in an online gallery.
