Secret Body

Dallis King, Samantha Puder, Nichie Enriquez, Anna Papazian

April 2015

Secret Body is an interactive art installation based around the idea of measuring and displaying human emotion. The piece uses bioinformatic data to create a light pattern within ten human-shaped models, and uses the same data to create an interactive platform between the piece and the audience.

Secret Body has won multiple awards including the "Most Creative" prize from the Canadian Undergraduate Software Engineering Conference in Montreal and "People's Choice" from the York University Undergraduate Research Fair, both in 2015. It was featured in the March 2015 create.InTo talk and has been shown at the Digital Media Showcase at York 2015 and Digifest 2015.
